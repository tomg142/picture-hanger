Desired Position

Gallery standard is 57" from floor to center of the art. Adjust to your eye level or preference.

Hanger Type

Flip the frame face-down. Measure from the top edge straight down to the center of the hook hole or D-ring slot.

How to measure the wire distance: Flip the hanging face-down. Pull the wire up from the center with one finger until it's taut. Measure from the top edge of the frame straight down to the highest point of the wire at your finger. That's your wire distance.

Measure from the top edge down to the center of the hook holes. Both hooks should be at the same height.
